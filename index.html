Why donate organs?
You are born with the ability to completely change someone's life!! Why waste it?
Don't think of organ donations as giving up a part of yourself to keep a total stranger alive.
It's really a total stranger giving up almost all of themselves to keep a part of you alive!!
The organ we donate might entirely change the life of the other person.
  • Imagine a little girl's heart pumping when her parents and friends are hopeless!
  • Imagine the happiness a blind person would get seeing the whole world with your eyes! Isn't it great?
    • Let's donate our organs once it is of no use to us. We not only help a person but also a part of us to relive.
